1. The Art of Imagery (圖像)

  • In the hospitality industry (酒店業), an image speaks a thousand words.
  • Photos must perfectly reflect (反映) the event you are promoting.
  • Example: A luxury spa requires calming imagery, whilst a festive gala (節慶晚宴) needs vibrant (充滿活力) photos.

Golden Rule:

Always ask yourself: "Does this image convey (傳達) the exact emotion and standard of our hotel?"

3. The Perfect Arrangement (排版)

A. Contrast (對比度)

  • You do not need a black background, you just need strong contrast.
  • Dark text on a light background (or vice versa) works perfectly.
  • If contrast is poor, our guests cannot read the information.

B. Visual Hierarchy (視覺層次)

  • Organise your information logically (有邏輯地).
  • The most important detail (Title) must be the largest.
  • Secondary details (次要細節) like Time and Venue should be smaller. Do not make everything the same size!
